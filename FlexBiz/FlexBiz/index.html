Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping with Velvet & Bloom.

Are all your products cruelty-free and vegan?

Yes! Every single product we sell is 100% cruelty-free and certified by Leaping Bunny. The vast majority of our range is also fully vegan. Any products containing beeswax or carmine are clearly labelled on the product page.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days. Express shipping (1–2 business days) is available at checkout. Orders over $50 qualify for free standard shipping. You'll receive tracking information via email as soon as your order is dispatched.

Can I return or exchange a product?

Absolutely. We offer a 30-day return policy on all unopened products. If a product arrived damaged or you experienced an allergic reaction, we'll happily accept returns on opened items too. Just contact our team at hello@velvetandbloom.com and we'll sort it out.

Are your products suitable for sensitive skin?

Our formulas are dermatologist-tested and free from common irritants like parabens, sulfates, synthetic fragrances and alcohol. That said, skin sensitivities vary — always patch test a new product and check the full ingredient list on each product page.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes! At checkout you can add complimentary gift wrapping with a handwritten note. Our packaging is already beautiful, but for gift orders we include a satin ribbon, tissue paper and a personalised card.

How do I find my right foundation shade?

Each foundation product page includes a detailed shade guide with images across a range of skin tones. You can also order our free Shade Sample Kit — just contact us and we'll send you a selection of mini samples to try at home before you commit.
